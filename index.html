Customer Lifecycle & Reverification Engine

An AI-native KYC reverification platform for a UAE digital bank. It identifies when and what needs to be reviewed, resolves the majority of cases automatically through trusted UAE data sources (ICA, UAE PASS, AECB), and reserves human compliance effort for the cases that genuinely matter - covering risk-based reverification, automated identity verification, sanctions & PEP screening, and a compliance review queue with a full, immutable audit trail.
