Product Case Study

Customer Lifecycle & Reverification Engine

An AI-native KYC reverification platform for a UAE digital bank. It intelligently identifies when and what needs to be reviewed, resolves the majority of cases automatically through trusted UAE data sources (ICA, UAE PASS, AECB), and reserves human compliance effort for the cases where it genuinely matters - covering risk-based reverification, automated identity verification, sanctions & PEP screening, and a compliance review queue with a full, immutable audit trail.

The Problem

Reverification in UAE digital banking consistently breaks down in the same ways:

• One process for everyone - a retired saver goes through the same review as a self-employed consultant receiving high-value international transfers, even though their risk profiles are entirely different.
• Manual work systems should own - compliance teams chase documents, sort and prioritise cases, and decide reviews that Emirates ID, AECB and UAE PASS could resolve automatically.
• Customers disrupted for no good reason - uniform document requests create friction for the low-risk majority, eroding trust and increasing support load.
• Audit trails as a liability - decisions captured in spreadsheets and email threads are hard to defend as the bank scales.
• Risk events caught too late - employment changes, unusual transactions and adverse-media hits surface through support tickets instead of triggering immediate action.

Goals

• Automate the majority of reverification with no customer or operations touch - using Emirates ID/ICA, UAE PASS and AECB to silently close low-risk periodic reviews and document refreshes. Target: 70%+ closed without human intervention.
• Make review frequency proportionate to risk - high-risk every 6 months, standard every 12, low-risk every 24 - replacing a non-compliant single annual cycle.
• Cover the full lifecycle - periodic reviews, event-driven re-KYC, document refresh, profile changes, source-of-funds re-collection, risk re-rating, lifecycle comms and escalation.
• Prepare every escalated case for the human - a structured evidence pack, AI-generated summary and recommended action so reviewers decide rather than assemble.
• Audit-ready by design - every automated resolution, customer submission and reviewer action written to an immutable, timestamped audit trail.

Key Capabilities

Dynamic Risk Profiles

Per-customer rating, review frequency and open triggers, recalculated by a configurable rules engine the compliance team can tune without an engineering change.

Trigger Detection & Consolidation

Scheduled, document-expiry, event-driven and customer-initiated triggers - deduplicated into a single case so the customer is contacted once, not many times.

Automated Resolution

ICA + UAE PASS + AECB cross-checks silently close low-risk cases when signals agree and a configurable confidence threshold is met; conflicts escalate.

Customer-Facing Journey

Bilingual (Arabic-first) flow triggered only when action is genuinely required, asking only for what the specific trigger needs - no generic KYC pack.

Compliance Review Queue

Prioritised queue with SLA countdowns, AI case summaries, four-eyes control on high-risk decisions, and a logged rationale for every outcome.

Screening & Audit

Continuous sanctions/PEP/adverse-media screening that freezes matches within minutes, plus an immutable audit log with regulator-ready CBUAE & FATF exports.

Approach & Architecture

A layered, modular design where data flows from ingestion through to the customer or reviewer, routed down one of three paths: auto-resolved, customer action required, or compliance review required. AI sits in the augmentation layer to summarise and recommend - it never makes the compliance decision; rules and humans govern decisions. Vendor selection was scoped for the UAE regulatory context (ICA Direct API, UAE PASS via Digital Dubai, AECB + Refinitiv World-Check, Appian case management, Azure Immutable Blob Storage for tamper-evident audit logging).

Execution Roadmap

A nine-month, two-phase plan delivered with a cross-functional team (engineering, product, design, compliance & legal):

• Phase 1 - Foundation & Working Compliance Operation (Months 1-3): risk profile engine, trigger engine, auto-resolution via ICA/UAE PASS/AECB, customer journey v1, review queue with four-eyes control, immutable audit log and a 1,000-customer controlled pilot - gated by a CBUAE regulatory review checkpoint.
• Phase 2 - Scale, Enrich & Strengthen Controls (Months 3-6+): continuous real-time screening, event-driven triggers, full source-of-funds re-collection, dormancy management, an Enhanced Due Diligence workflow and enhanced (WhatsApp/Braze) communications.

Delivery principle: regulatory validation before scale - Phase 2 does not begin until the automated-decisioning approach passes the CBUAE checkpoint.
